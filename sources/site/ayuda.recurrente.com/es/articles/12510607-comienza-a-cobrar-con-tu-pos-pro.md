# Paso 1: Conéctate a internet

1. Mantén presionado el botón de encendido de tu POS durante 3 segundos para encenderlo. 
 ​

 [![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759173847/2d41b18acd3d37ebaaf106d80bf4/Boton+de+encendido.png?expires=1784052900&signature=22d1dca865386df31f5bf20f5e4eda2b3b6eeecb43f2c6d5a30f6abdf11a348c&req=dSciH8h5nolbXvMW1HO4zQRhJqG3y6qL0D9Js6kw1nzb4VLaPhYq9bMaNoM2%0AoCK%2B%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759173847/2d41b18acd3d37ebaaf106d80bf4/Boton+de+encendido.png?expires=1784052900&signature=22d1dca865386df31f5bf20f5e4eda2b3b6eeecb43f2c6d5a30f6abdf11a348c&req=dSciH8h5nolbXvMW1HO4zQRhJqG3y6qL0D9Js6kw1nzb4VLaPhYq9bMaNoM2%0AoCK%2B%0A)

2. Conecta el POS a tu red Wifi. Para esto, ingresa a "**Configuración**" y luego a "**Internet y redes**". 
 ​

 [![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1764552880/49bcf1e71b06c26c329991bc8551/Internet+y+redes.png?expires=1784052900&signature=82752f62dea40f1879ed68b08daa17c81630715cfe514b9cefd8ef3174adbf68&req=dSchEsx7n4lXWfMW1HO4zcZAMM370q3s3h1BLQcyC1TabJbZSN2QWPLev4VZ%0APk%2Ff%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1764552880/49bcf1e71b06c26c329991bc8551/Internet+y+redes.png?expires=1784052900&signature=82752f62dea40f1879ed68b08daa17c81630715cfe514b9cefd8ef3174adbf68&req=dSchEsx7n4lXWfMW1HO4zcZAMM370q3s3h1BLQcyC1TabJbZSN2QWPLev4VZ%0APk%2Ff%0A)

# Paso 2: Configura tu cuenta

1. Ingresa a la aplicación de Recurrente.

 [![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1811386050/320ab089bd4836d49a1715cb73b5/New-app-pos.png?expires=1784052900&signature=895a9385d3a388aae78949ec6a18ba786ed1b0941f5f620d05bdc911fafe45dc&req=dSgmF8p2m4FaWfMW1HO4zc4yybIhLudZR4eRu7bHti100X6siUYLOlVxR%2B8b%0ASMLK%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1811386050/320ab089bd4836d49a1715cb73b5/New-app-pos.png?expires=1784052900&signature=895a9385d3a388aae78949ec6a18ba786ed1b0941f5f620d05bdc911fafe45dc&req=dSgmF8p2m4FaWfMW1HO4zc4yybIhLudZR4eRu7bHti100X6siUYLOlVxR%2B8b%0ASMLK%0A)

2\. Inicia sesión con tu cuenta.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759304821/592a2fbf3b9f8f4fce5a447138e8/IMG_8404.jpg?expires=1784052900&signature=87c764b0c67a8206d6aebaceec458ef841d520f43042cd3a76dfe72a345a108e&req=dSciH8p%2BmYldWPMW1HO4zV6i%2F7ZGZxY0hsSrUuZNGw6i1aeKLvko2IRo1GZy%0A%2B%2FE12X53CI5RgEuwmrY%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759304821/592a2fbf3b9f8f4fce5a447138e8/IMG_8404.jpg?expires=1784052900&signature=87c764b0c67a8206d6aebaceec458ef841d520f43042cd3a76dfe72a345a108e&req=dSciH8p%2BmYldWPMW1HO4zV6i%2F7ZGZxY0hsSrUuZNGw6i1aeKLvko2IRo1GZy%0A%2B%2FE12X53CI5RgEuwmrY%3D%0A)

3\. Escoge la cuenta del negocio que usarás para cobrar con el POS.

**💡 Tip importante:** Tu POS Pro puede utilizarse en varias cuentas de Recurrente. Para cambiar de cuenta, solo necesitas iniciar sesión con el correo asociado a la cuenta que deseas utilizar.

# Paso 3: Realiza un cobro

1. Ingresa el monto que deseas cobrar y presiona el botón de "**Cobrar**".

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759325275/6db11a6ed91c7db5da829673881d/POS+Cobrar+2.png?expires=1784052900&signature=a2fea5f7482871f9308b7b0e864d7e69acf9b908ce50f899c75d8c72b636ddc4&req=dSciH8p8mINYXPMW1HO4zfsvslpc6leU2Wi53hhLFDcAG%2BicVplcChIbzH3R%0AMEi0Nh2e0I%2BNEJN8aXY%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759325275/6db11a6ed91c7db5da829673881d/POS+Cobrar+2.png?expires=1784052900&signature=a2fea5f7482871f9308b7b0e864d7e69acf9b908ce50f899c75d8c72b636ddc4&req=dSciH8p8mINYXPMW1HO4zfsvslpc6leU2Wi53hhLFDcAG%2BicVplcChIbzH3R%0AMEi0Nh2e0I%2BNEJN8aXY%3D%0A)

2\. Selecciona si deseas hacer el cobro al contado o en cuotas. Luego, presiona "**Cobrar**". 
​

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759342774/8cb33bc5963e694e1c6d80f87daf/Contado+o+cuotas+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=d5092844b4da7d84c41e2dc4264fa6f6d55dc7cf66db4ccf4a9eff286387ea54&req=dSciH8p6n4ZYXfMW1HO4zaQm1KNtySlxVdiv6ARyOuS8pVlo5LkzILB6oU9N%0A4bDSJCJPZgEZdvUZxpk%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759342774/8cb33bc5963e694e1c6d80f87daf/Contado+o+cuotas+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=d5092844b4da7d84c41e2dc4264fa6f6d55dc7cf66db4ccf4a9eff286387ea54&req=dSciH8p6n4ZYXfMW1HO4zaQm1KNtySlxVdiv6ARyOuS8pVlo5LkzILB6oU9N%0A4bDSJCJPZgEZdvUZxpk%3D%0A)

3\. Acerca la tarjeta de tu cliente al lector del POS para realizar el cobro. 
​

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759392434/54398c81ba73b201044fc0e963be/POS+Pro+con+tarjeta.png?expires=1784052900&signature=3f4d672b07773a4800ad329dc2091519ae171d30958da86b3aa01c5f0b4a2491&req=dSciH8p3n4VcXfMW1HO4zRO%2F7vFIjl5n%2FcWtj29gMEWWZ0y5zLPiSBGNizN%2B%0Aufc%2BdeYivyqKOBLODn8%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1759392434/54398c81ba73b201044fc0e963be/POS+Pro+con+tarjeta.png?expires=1784052900&signature=3f4d672b07773a4800ad329dc2091519ae171d30958da86b3aa01c5f0b4a2491&req=dSciH8p3n4VcXfMW1HO4zRO%2F7vFIjl5n%2FcWtj29gMEWWZ0y5zLPiSBGNizN%2B%0Aufc%2BdeYivyqKOBLODn8%3D%0A)

¡Listo! Ya realizaste tu primer cobro con tu POS.

# Paso 4: Envía o imprime el comprobante de pago

1. Para **envío digital** del comprobante, puedes enviarlo por Whatsapp a tu cliente o por medio de Email. 
 ​

2. Para **impresión** del comprobante, presiona el botón de "**Imprimir comprobante**". 
 ​ 
 ​

 [![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1768614464/808131c1157fe605a913e61b11c5/Imprimir+comprobante.png?expires=1784052900&signature=0128b765e8e1b0e575f7920e9d1f726e44615fab48a50a1f3e6653d95f74484a&req=dSchHs9%2FmYVZXfMW1HO4za3wYh4bEEh1Os1kZu%2Bv5VBkiCXrJr1U47nLfk8t%0AfNTj%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1768614464/808131c1157fe605a913e61b11c5/Imprimir+comprobante.png?expires=1784052900&signature=0128b765e8e1b0e575f7920e9d1f726e44615fab48a50a1f3e6653d95f74484a&req=dSchHs9%2FmYVZXfMW1HO4za3wYh4bEEh1Os1kZu%2Bv5VBkiCXrJr1U47nLfk8t%0AfNTj%0A)

 ​

3. Si quisieras imprimir el comprobante después de haber concluido la transacción, puedes buscar la transacción en la sección de "Actividad", presionar "..." y seleccionar "**Imprimir comprobante cliente**". 
 ​

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1768620300/ad190ee9145714c855fa3f04555f/Comprobante+impresion+actividad.png?expires=1784052900&signature=045c388faec36c2e8ce991d7f71b0ab7a38b812b7b457643e5169acc2a020dec&req=dSchHs98nYJfWfMW1HO4zfA2OCEtkAeA5IgBuzuP2iljYv%2BQLEm4b2XbMPbQ%0AKeKMfssbPwZDkEtR7wE%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1768620300/ad190ee9145714c855fa3f04555f/Comprobante+impresion+actividad.png?expires=1784052900&signature=045c388faec36c2e8ce991d7f71b0ab7a38b812b7b457643e5169acc2a020dec&req=dSchHs98nYJfWfMW1HO4zfA2OCEtkAeA5IgBuzuP2iljYv%2BQLEm4b2XbMPbQ%0AKeKMfssbPwZDkEtR7wE%3D%0A)
