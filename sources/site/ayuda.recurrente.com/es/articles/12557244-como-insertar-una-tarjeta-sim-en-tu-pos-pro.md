# ¿Cómo insertar una tarjeta SIM en tu POS Pro?

Pasos para insertar una tarjeta SIM para usar tus datos celulares con el POS.

20 de mayo de 2026

**Importante:** Tu tarjeta SIM debe ser de un tamaño estándar 15mm x 25mm, si tienes un Micro SIM o un Nano SIM, utiliza un adaptador de tamaño.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771956200/0b2f17d8454f69ac1be902e66dee/Sim%2Bcard%2Bsizes.jpeg?expires=1784052900&signature=05ee421110141dc82fe79b396cfb11eae5e48970636ea348e1ab266af1bb7566&req=dScgF8B7m4NfWfMW1HO4zYTg7ej1tXNg%2FTA2yHTlQbAE3%2BgpWs5lfxb1Y0QD%0ABPYFm2d0QS9GcsyfTCU%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771956200/0b2f17d8454f69ac1be902e66dee/Sim%2Bcard%2Bsizes.jpeg?expires=1784052900&signature=05ee421110141dc82fe79b396cfb11eae5e48970636ea348e1ab266af1bb7566&req=dScgF8B7m4NfWfMW1HO4zYTg7ej1tXNg%2FTA2yHTlQbAE3%2BgpWs5lfxb1Y0QD%0ABPYFm2d0QS9GcsyfTCU%3D%0A)

1. Apaga tu POS, presionando el botón lateral y seleccionando la opción de "**Apagar**" en la pantalla.

 ​

 [![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771844010/e122131fa7cf756043aaa39af515/Boton%2Bde%2Bencendido.png?expires=1784052900&signature=f02fab85596d42d047dca78127416a43d625564fb3138d56494555c57d786ceb&req=dScgF8F6mYFeWfMW1HO4zVi5%2B5v1EK6OztaH9cK2cCBlCP9J6HXrfU18ODeI%0ANKBv%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771844010/e122131fa7cf756043aaa39af515/Boton%2Bde%2Bencendido.png?expires=1784052900&signature=f02fab85596d42d047dca78127416a43d625564fb3138d56494555c57d786ceb&req=dScgF8F6mYFeWfMW1HO4zVi5%2B5v1EK6OztaH9cK2cCBlCP9J6HXrfU18ODeI%0ANKBv%0A)

2. Desbloquea la parte trasera del POS.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771914156/7a3485044590dbdc0284d3314e38/Unlock+back+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=2b12db7b47d4c993b00a27c48b4bf47aa2e7ddd0efffa721bba5dfc1e016d952&req=dScgF8B%2FmYBaX%2FMW1HO4zX9hxOeSW%2BBR0ijZxPq%2BST%2BsdNQSUM4WQ%2FQvpsVO%0A8GCq39W6cSYm4zMzrfY%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771914156/7a3485044590dbdc0284d3314e38/Unlock+back+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=2b12db7b47d4c993b00a27c48b4bf47aa2e7ddd0efffa721bba5dfc1e016d952&req=dScgF8B%2FmYBaX%2FMW1HO4zX9hxOeSW%2BBR0ijZxPq%2BST%2BsdNQSUM4WQ%2FQvpsVO%0A8GCq39W6cSYm4zMzrfY%3D%0A)

3\. Levanta la tapadera trasera desde la abertura lateral.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771925483/7db5701d6b6a337529b43af41e0a/Tapadera+back+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=2117b280338d82b9f45fed239a5bed0606d5970d9c0d62df6b244023460a41bd&req=dScgF8B8mIVXWvMW1HO4zXZeDtK5QZRUDM8Kiewl4VXbscfu3STQXbd2KaN2%0AkwW%2FTvlOHGEAJ7y4BPg%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771925483/7db5701d6b6a337529b43af41e0a/Tapadera+back+POS.png?expires=1784052900&signature=2117b280338d82b9f45fed239a5bed0606d5970d9c0d62df6b244023460a41bd&req=dScgF8B8mIVXWvMW1HO4zXZeDtK5QZRUDM8Kiewl4VXbscfu3STQXbd2KaN2%0AkwW%2FTvlOHGEAJ7y4BPg%3D%0A)

4\. Retira la batería e inserta la tarjeta SIM en la dirección correcta y en el compartimiento inferior indicado.

[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771980284/55d0785efb8f25df4c7e7d093a24/SIM+POS+inside.png?expires=1784052900&signature=973395a6c182fcec3369c1a6412236d0cf7f08810aa6a64c1e3598b081ebc381&req=dScgF8B2nYNXXfMW1HO4zUayTnf3%2Ftqkr7FmwHQN%2FrO%2BJ2iKJ2izD0VtMNL8%0AdfUI7Xc4TEzjLSQAYP8%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/q0how4fq/1771980284/55d0785efb8f25df4c7e7d093a24/SIM+POS+inside.png?expires=1784052900&signature=973395a6c182fcec3369c1a6412236d0cf7f08810aa6a64c1e3598b081ebc381&req=dScgF8B2nYNXXfMW1HO4zUayTnf3%2Ftqkr7FmwHQN%2FrO%2BJ2iKJ2izD0VtMNL8%0AdfUI7Xc4TEzjLSQAYP8%3D%0A)

5\. ¡Listo! Enciende el POS y espera 2 minutos en lo que el dispositivo comienza a recibir la señal celular.
